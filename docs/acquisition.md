# Receipt Sorter Acquisition Plan

## Current Judgment

Do not start paid ads as the first move. The product is public, but the conversion path is still too early: no analytics, no payment flow, and no proof that visitors understand the job-to-be-done.

Start with low-cost user acquisition that can tell us whether people will click, try the sample, and paste their own notes.

## First Measurement Target

- Landing page visitor -> app open
- App open -> sample run
- Sample run -> own notes attempt
- Own notes attempt -> CSV/checklist action
- Qualitative feedback: "I would use this before month-end accounting" or "This does not match my workflow"

## This Week's Free / Low-Cost Channels

1. X short demo post
   - Goal: See if the Before/After problem framing gets clicks.
   - Post idea: "メルカリ副業の月末メモ、売上・送料・梱包材が混ざると地味につらいので、確認リストに分ける無料デモを作りました。写真アップロードなし、税務判断なし。"
   - CTA: "サンプルだけ30秒で見られます。"

2. note build article
   - Goal: Explain the target workflow and collect readers who recognize the pain.
   - Title idea: "メルカリ副業の月末メモ整理を、CSVではなく確認リストとして作った理由"
   - CTA: "無料デモで、自分のメモに近いか確認してください。"

3. Handmade / small seller community post
   - Goal: Validate whether minne/BOOTH/mercari sellers have the same month-end memo problem.
   - Post idea: "販売メモの月末整理で、売上・送料・梱包材が混ざる人向けの無料デモを置きました。確定申告ツールではなく、会計入力前の下書きです。"

4. Manual feedback request to 5-10 likely users
   - Goal: Get direct friction notes before ads.
   - Ask: "サンプルを見て、自分のメモでも試す気になるかだけ教えてください。"

## Paid Ads Test, Only After Basic Measurement

Preferred first paid test: Google Search Ads, not Meta or X.

Reason:
- The product is tied to an active intent: people searching for ways to manage Mercari / small seller sales, shipping, fees, and month-end notes.
- Meta can work for visual Before/After later, but it may produce cheap low-intent traffic before the product has conversion tracking.
- X can work for founder-led organic posts first, but paid X traffic is risky without a strong account or a proven post to amplify.

Small test:
- Budget: JPY 3,000-7,000 total for 5-7 days.
- Objective: website traffic first; conversion only after own-note execution can be tracked.
- Keywords: "メルカリ 売上管理", "メルカリ 送料 手数料 管理", "メルカリ 確定申告 売上 整理", "メルカリ 副業 経費 管理".
- Success: meaningful clicks plus sample-run and own-note conversion, not purchases.

Ad copy draft:

> メルカリ副業の月末メモ、売上・送料・梱包材が混ざっていませんか。  
> 写真アップロードなし。税務判断なし。  
> まずは無料サンプルで、確認リストになる流れを見てください。

Do not:
- Claim tax filing automation.
- Claim AI, OCR, or accounting accuracy.
- Target broad "副業" only.
- Use Mercari logos or imply official partnership.
- Spend before basic click/sample/own-note actions can be measured.
